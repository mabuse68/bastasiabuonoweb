Conscious cookies that taste plain delicious

BastaSiaBuono is an ethical bakery that is the antidote to all eating regrets. Our maître pâtissier reworks traditional Italian recipes to create a range of animal- and dairy-free classic cookies.

The handmade produce is delivered oven-fresh to a pick-up point near you. These pick-up points include specialty tea/coffee shops and artisanal food producers, and we keep a curated list of their products that go best with our freshly-made cookies.


"Basta Sia Buono"

is a Tuscan saying that means “good food is all that matters”.

Our products


Occhio di Bue

A light and delicious sable cookie filled with a dollop of Sicilian apricot puree and dashed with super-fine veil sugar. The perfect addition to any vegan tea break.

Cecino

Made by slowly baking our own hand-ground chickpea dough, which is bound with cane sugar and dipped in Dutched cacao, these low-fat cookies bring a surprisingly characterful flavour to that precious bit of “me-time”.

Befanino

There’s a kid in all of us – and this is every Italian kid’s favourite Christmas cookie. A slowly baked shortbread, flavoured with anise seeds and cut into playful shapes.

Bruttino-choc

It’s ugly, it’s vegan, it’s a chocolate explosion! A cacao-scented blob mixing a crumbly, rich texture with voluptuous Dutched cocoa sensations.

Bruttino-ginger

It’s ugly, it’s vegan, and it tastes great! A crumbly, rich blob flavoured with fresh ginger and lime.
