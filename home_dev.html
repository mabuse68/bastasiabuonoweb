About BastaSiaBuono


Conscious cookies that taste plain delicious

BastaSiaBuono is the conscious bakery for everyone that keeps eating regrets at bay. Our maître pâtissier reinterprets recipes at the root of the Italian popular tradition, making animal and dairy-free cookie-classics.

The produce is made by hand and delivered oven-fresh to a pick-up point near you. We select our pick-up points among specialty tea/coffee shops and artisanal food producers and we keep a curated list of the best products they offer that match your freshly-made cookies.


"Basta Sia Buono"

It's a vernacular Tuscan way of saying that good food it's all that matters.

Our products


Occhio di Bue, sweet bullseye

The Vegan tea-time companion, light and delicious Sable cookie, center-filled with a dollop of puree of Sicilian Apricots and dashed with super-fine veil sugar.

Cecino: chickpea surprise

Low-fat cookies, made by slowly baking our own hand-ground chickpea dough, bound with cane sugar and dipped in dutched cacao; they bring a surprisingly characterful taste to your pause.

Befanino: child memories

There's a kid in everyone of us and this is the kids' cookie when Xmas comes in Italy. A slowly baked shortbread, flavoured with anise seeds and cut in a playful form.

Bruttino-choc: ugly but tasty

It's ugly, it's vegan, it's a chocolate explosion! Cacao-scented blob mixing crumbly, rich texture with voluptuous dutched cocoa sensations.

Bruttino-ginger: spicy and crispy

It's ugly, it's vegan, it tastes great! Perfumed with fresh ginger and lime, a crumbly blob with rich texture.

Occhio di Bue chickpea lo-cal

Slowly baked hand-ground chickpea dough rings filled with a dollop of apricot puree and dashed with super-fine veil sugar.
